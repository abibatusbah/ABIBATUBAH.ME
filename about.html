About Me

This is what I do.

Abibatu Bah is a Sierraleonean, born in the northern provience of Sierra Leone. Abibatu is a student of Limkokwing University of Creative Technology Sierra Leone. A staff at the TpISENT (S.L) Limited, as a Graphic Designer and a digital photographyer. Abibatu Bah, is reliable, honest, skilled and motivated individual with a background in Multimedia, advertising, broadcasting, Web Design, Digital graphics, 2D Animation, Broadcast Media Production, Consumer production, web technology, Digital imaging, programming, Adobe pakage, (photoshope, Illustrator & Flash), Graphic Design (HTML & CSS), basic knowledge on java script, Bootstrap, good communicator with unique ethical behaviour and perseverance skills and the ability to adapt to new environments. I have the ability to work under pressure and remain confident in what ever situation. Flexible and well organised. Fluent in English, Fullah, Themne and Krio languages to be specific.
